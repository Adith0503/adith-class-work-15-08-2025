Intro to Web Foundations

Course Syllabus

Overview

This course introduces the fundamental principles of web design and development using HTML, CSS, and accessibility best practices.

By the end of the course, students will be able to create structured, semantic, and visually appealing web pages. “Academic integrity is the foundation of learning and discovery.” – University Academic Policy
